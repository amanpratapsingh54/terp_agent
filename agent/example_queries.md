# TerpAgent — Example Prompts

Drop these into the Claude Cowork chat (with the TerpAgent skill active) to exercise every branch of the API.

## Daily orientation
- "Morning check-in"
- "What's on fire today?"
- "Anything due in the next 48 hours?"

## Academic
- "Which CMSC422 assignment should I start tonight? How much is it worth?"
- "Who's the TA for 414 and when are their office hours?"
- "Draft an email to Prof. Daume about joining CLIP this summer."

## Jobs
- "Best TA positions I can actually get for fall"
- "Rank the top 3 SWE internships on Handshake for me and tell me what I'd have to fix in my resume for each"
- "Any part-time on-campus job that fits around my Tue/Thu schedule?"

## Events + social
- "What's worth doing this weekend? Prefer tech-ish + free."
- "Any of my friends going to the ACM hack night?"
- "Who in my CMSC414 section would be a good study buddy?"

## Housing
- "Find me 3 off-campus places under $1,100 within a 12-minute walk of Iribe"
- "Help me match with a roommate and a 2BR for next year"

## Scholarships
- "Which scholarships should I apply to before the semester ends?"
- "Track the CMNS one and remind me to start the essay."

## Professors & research
- "Which profs should I take a class with next semester given my ML + security interests?"
- "Draft research outreach to the top 2 matches."

## Travel
- "Find me something to do off-campus Saturday afternoon — no car."
- "Weekend trip under 2.5 hours?"

## Library
- "Book me a 4-person room in McKeldin tomorrow 2pm for 2 hours."
- "What books do I have on hold?"
